About Me

Hello! I am Yilin Li. I am a Junior and aspiring UX researcher pursuing an Informatics B.S with an HCI specialization at Indiana University. I am also minoring in Web Development and Psychology. As a woman of color in a tech reliant world, I am passionate about creating digital environments that benefit all. This leads to my involvement in Kappa Theta Pi at IU as a member of Standards and as a merch committee member.

When I am not in class or participating in my extracurriculars, I can be found junk journaling with a matcha next to me, doing nails or curled up in my bed reading a thrilling book. I love fun drinks, strawberry short cake, cats and the color green!
